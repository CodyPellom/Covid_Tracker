About

This site uses an API that tracks Covid-19 cases daily in the United States. As time goes on I will update the site to have global stats and more information about possible solutions. While this information may be helpful in us tracking the virus, it is not accurate to the extent of tracking the cases that are going unreported. You can check this site daily to keep informed.

This site is intended to help Americans understand the extent of the virus without any media bias. These are the straight numbers.
